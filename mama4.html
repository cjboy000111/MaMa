To My Mama 💐

Mama, you may not have been there for every little moment while I was growing up. You may not have been the one teaching me how to drive, how to cook, how to do laundry, or signing every school paper.

But none of those things ever changed the fact that you loved me. Not even distance could change that.

You sacrificed comfort, time, and so many quiet moments just so I could have a better future. While I was growing up, you were out there working hard, carrying responsibilities most people will never fully understand.

And even if life made us far apart, I still carry your love with me every single day. In the way I keep trying. In the way I keep fighting. In the way I still believe better days will come.

Sometimes I wonder how hard it must have been for you. Missing birthdays. Missing ordinary days. Missing moments a mother should have been there for. Yet you still kept going for me.

Mama, thank you for never giving up on our future. Thank you for every sacrifice nobody saw. Thank you for loving me from afar.

I may not say it enough, but I am proud to be your son. And one day, I promise all your sacrifices will be worth it.

No matter how far we are from each other, you will always be my home.
